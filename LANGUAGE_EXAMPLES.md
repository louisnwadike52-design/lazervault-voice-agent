## Pronunciation Guide

### Igbo Special Characters
- **á»‹** = sounds like "i" in "pit"
- **á»¥** = sounds like "u" in "put"
- **á»** = sounds like "o" in "pot"
- **á¹…** = nasal "ng" sound
- **gb** = combined "g" and "b" sound

### Hausa Special Characters
- **Æ™** = emphatic "k" sound
- **É—** = implosive "d" sound
- **É“** = implosive "b" sound

### Yoruba Special Characters & Tones
- **áº¹** = sounds like "e" in "bet"
